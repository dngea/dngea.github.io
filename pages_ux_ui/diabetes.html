    Web application to manage patients suffering from diabetes type 1 for hospital departement in Porto.
    June of 2023
    Project carried on by a group of 12 members from three different masters in the University of Porto: Multimedia, Computer sciences and Product management.
    As a member of the multimedia team, we conducted the user research, studied trends for the web design and did research on diabetes type 1 case studies with previous developed applications (market research).
    Figma was adopted as the main software to develop the design and to communicate the ideas for the web to our colleagues from the IT team.
    Technical rider
    Software: Figma, Davinci Resolve, After effects. (frontEnd and backEnd frameworks).
    Reources: Research papers, Users' feedback.
    Idea: Design, prototyping, inclusion, tech, innovation.
    Location: University of Porto.
