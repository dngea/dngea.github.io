    Mobile app for department of inclusion of the University of Porto.
    NAI (Núcleo de apoio à inclução).
    June of 2023
    This project consisted on the development of a mobile application for the department of inclusion of the Univerisity of Porto.
    Despite the fact that it might seems obvious the target audience, it happened to be a surprise for our group when we encountered that still this point was not clear yet even for the members of the department. As it was still an up-and-coming project.

    For this reasons, this project involved all the stages of the User research process (Personas, interaction scenarios, task analysis and conceptual model). Consecutively, we designed the interfaces on Figma (wireframes and prototypes). When the lo-fi prototype was ready, we tested it with a firt "Piloto test". We collected the feedback and the first hi-fi prototype was ready to test it with a battery of potential users, specially focused on users with visual impairment.
    Technical rider
    Software: Figma.
    Reources: Users' feedback, free images
    Idea: Design, prototyping, inclusion, tech, innovation.
    Location: University of Porto, NAI
