    web development
    I came across my passion for web design and development by accident, if I am allowed to say that. I have been always attracted by photography since I was a child and at some point in my life, after making some public presentations in physical spaces, I decided to dive into other digital possibilities such as websites to show my work.

    I did not have any idea of how webs were made at that time, some basic knowledge of HTML and protocols from high school but nothing really solid. But when I found my purpose to learn web development, which was experimenting with different media to show my photographs, I got captivated by the potential and possibilities of the web.
