    • About
    I was born in the south of Spain, in a city called Granada. I lived there until the age of 19 and then I started to live abroad, an experience which made me grow in a personal and professional way.

    As of today, I am a Multimedia Master's student at the University of Porto with a background in Information Sciences. I am actively engaged in projects that allow me to apply my skills and knowledge of multimedia design to solve complex problems and interact with diverse teams. I am passionate about creating innovative multimedia experiences. Mixing visuals, sound, lights, interfaces and controls that captivate audiences and push the boundaries of what is possible.

    Along my academic journey, I have had the privilege of studying at institutions such as the University of Granada, Salamanca, Coimbra and Porto. I have needed to face and solve new problems and adapt myself to new environments, nevertheless and with no doubts, the biggest challenge I have come across in the last years was: the rain of Porto.
