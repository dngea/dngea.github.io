    I was born in the south of Spain, in a city called Granada. I lived there until my age of 19 and then I started to live abroad, experience which made me grow in a personal and professional way.

    As of today, I am a Multimedia Master's student at the University of Porto with a background in Information Sciences. Along my academic journey, I have had the privilege of studying at other esteemed institutions such as the University of Granada, Salamanca and Coimbra, expanding my academic horizons and intercultural competences.

    Currently, I am actively engaged in projects that allow me to apply my skills and knowledge of multimedia design to solve complex problems and interact with diverse teams. I am passionate about creating innovative multimedia experiences that captivate audiences and push the boundaries of what is possible.
