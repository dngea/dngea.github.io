I came across my passion for web development, and eventually UX/UI design, almost by accident. Since childhood, I've always been drawn to photography, and after showcasing my work in physical spaces through public presentations, I felt inspired to explore digital platforms like websites to display my art.

At the time, I had no real knowledge of how websites were built—just some basic understanding of HTML and web protocols from high school. But once I found a purpose for learning web development—experimenting with new ways to present my photographs—I became captivated by the endless possibilities the web offered.
