UX/UI

I discovered my passion for UX/UI design almost by accident. My journey began with a desire to present my art in more engaging and effective ways. This curiosity led me to move deeper into understanding how users interact with digital platforms.

I believe that great design is rooted in a methodical approach, emphasising the importance of understanding user needs. Through methodologies like personas, empathy maps, interviews, and usability testing, I thrive on creating intuitive and user-centered designs.
