A light Touch.
January of 2023
An installation that focuses on creating a non-verbal connection between two people, mostly strangers.
The installation will consist of two parts: sound and light. The sound reacts to the distance between two people, and the light to touch.
Our project seeks to emphasize the fact that sometimes you don't need a lot of words to make a connection, you just need to see and feel the other person, to acknowledge the presence of our surroundings.
Technical rider
Software: Arduino IDE, Processing, p5.js
Reources: Led lights, arduino boards, sensors, projector, curtains, speakers
Idea: Social interaction narrative where interactive media brings people together.
