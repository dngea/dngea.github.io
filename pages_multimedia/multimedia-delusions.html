Overview

The performance focuses on blending dark, emotional music with visuals that change and evolve. The music features haunting and atmospheric sounds that evoke a sense of nostalgia. The visuals are designed to match these feelings, creating a dreamy and mysterious atmosphere.

Project Goals

The project aims to deliver an engaging performance by integrating music and visuals seamlessly, providing a captivating experience for the audience.
