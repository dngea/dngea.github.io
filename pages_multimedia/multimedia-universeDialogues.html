Universe dialogues.
December of 2022
UNIVERSE DIALOGUE is a collaborative art experiment in which everyone can take part by sending their message into space. The message then lights up a bright star in our vast galaxy. Afterward, each participant can see how their small contribution affects the installation and be able to interact with it physically.
The project helps people become aware of their current emotional state and feel a connection with other people that tested our project before them. By our design, the installation becomes a kind of conduit to maintain a conversation with a higher being, a kind of medium through which people can finally be heard. Moreover, it is an emotional portrait of our society, helping people to feel a sense of belonging and connection with others.
Technical rider
Software: Arduino IDE, p5.js.
Reources: Arduino boards, sensors, projector, speakers.
Idea: Social interaction narrative where interactive media brings people together.
Location: University of Porto.
