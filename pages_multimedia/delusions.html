    Brutal delusions
    June of 2023
    Creation of a A/V (network) performance with interaction across A/V manifestations and control structures. This installation resulted in a music performance with visuals reacting to the music. The concept of this performance was to portray an obscure, dreamy, nostalgic ambient. Merging gloomy music with fitting changing visuals.
    Technical rider
    Software: Touchdesigner, Mixxx
    Reources: Commercial music & free images
    Idea: Brutalistic visuals and music reaction
    Location: University of Porto
