In medias res.
November of 2022
A reflective experience on the unfavourable effects of heavy information consumption.
The narrative-driven installation depicts how a constant flow of media can become overwhelming and addictive and interfere with our ability to connect to the present and to ourselves. The person will receive an increasing amount of information over a short period of time from different media outlets and a choice to turn it all off. Subsequently, a message will reveal itself encouraging the person to consider their choice and reflect on their priorities.
Technical rider
Software: Java, Figma, Davinci Resolve.
Resources: Circuits, Projector, spakers, headphones, mobile
Idea: Digital narrative project.
Location: University of Porto.
